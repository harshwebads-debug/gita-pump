About Gita Pumps


Gita Pump was established in the year 1964 as a Proprietorship firm by its Present Managing Director or Founder Mr. B.S. Saini, for the purpose of Designing, Manufacturing and Supplying of all types and sizes of Centrifugal, Mixed and Axial Flow Pumps for water and all other liquids. The company started on a small scale, and has since grown steadily. The Companys present scope comprises a very wide range of pumps in various sizes and for various applications. The companys product is supplied all over India and is exported to different countries.

An ISO 9001-2015 Company

ISO 9001 is a set of international standards for quality management and quality assurance. It provides a framework for organizations to implement quality management systems (QMS) and demonstrate their commitment to delivering quality products or services. At Gita Pumps, strict adherence to these global standards ensures consistency.

100% Client Satisfaction

Gita Pumps India Pvt. Ltd. has derived a robust system to measure customer satisfaction levels at regular intervals. The corrective actions and their effects are monitored religiously and regularly to ensure we exceed client expectations every time.

Quality Assurance

Being an ISO 9001:2015 Certified Organization, we follow stringent quality checking measures at each and every stage of the manufacturing process. From raw material procurement to final assembly, every step is scrutinized to ensure the delivery of flawless products.

Product Testing

Careful control and recording of flow rates during pumping tests are essential for obtaining high-quality results. Gita Pumps is equipped with state-of-the-art testing facilities to verify Head, Flow, and Efficiency parameters accurately before dispatch.

Technical Know-how

Our Founder Managing Director Mr. B.S. Saini is a Pump Designer of eminence and is among very few hydraulic pump designers in the country. He had obtained his B.Sc Engg. Mech. Degree with honors from Banaras Hindu University in the year 1956 and thereafter M.Sc. Engg. (Mech) Degree from the University of Michigan USA in the year 1959.

Thereafter he worked as a Pump Design Engineer in a Pump Co. of USA for three years. While working in this company, Mr. Saini acquired considerable knowledge and experience in the Designing and Development of all types and sizes of pumps from basic hydraulic design constants.

"We, at Gita Pumps design pumps from basic design constants as available in research manuals and as approved by American Hydraulic Institute, New York."
